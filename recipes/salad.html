Salad Recipe

Description

Eat healthy with this unique salad recipe which is sure to excite your tastebuds!

Ingredients

  • 12 slices bacon
  • 2 heads fresh broccoli, florets only
  • 1 cup chopped celery
  • 1/2 cup chopped green onions
  • 1 cup seedless green grapes
  • 1/2 cup raisins
  • 1/2 cup blanched slivered almonds
  • 1 cup mayonnaise
  • 1 tablespoon white wine vinegar
  • 1/4 cup white sugar
